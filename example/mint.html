Avime Stamps are a collection of 2500 anime PFP
avatars fully encoded on the Bitcoin blockchain using the
SRC-721 specification

This page was put up in haste because there is an ongoing feud between Stamps and XCP.

We am trying to get this project deployed before any potential fork of XCP occurs.
The first 2500 Avime Stamps that are minted and locked will be considered part of the collection

(see Cursed Ordinals)

Follow all 3 steps below to mint your Avime Stamp:
You must follow all 3 steps for your Avime Stamp to be valid!

Step #2: Use Freewallet.io to create a new token

Step #3: Use Freewallet.io to lock your stamp

After the stamp shows up in free wallet, lock the supply
